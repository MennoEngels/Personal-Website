LanguageLink - learning platform

Languagelink is a language learning platform that I'm currently developing using Django. My goal with this platform is to empower users to take control of their own language learning journey. With Languagelink, learners have access to a wide range of tools and resources to improve their language skills, including interactive lessons, different types of exercises and the ability to create their own custom lessons.

One of the primary objectives of Languagelink is to support self-directed learning. giving users the flexibility to choose how they want to learn and progress at their own pace. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner.

Languagelink will support multiple languages, making it accessible to learners from around the world. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, with a sleek and modern design.

By developing Languagelink, I was able to further enhance my skills in Django and web development. I am excited to continue developing this project and improving its features and functionality.
